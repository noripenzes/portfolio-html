The Best Movies According to Nori

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Cath me if you can

Catch Me If You Can is a 2002 American biographical film starring Leonardo DiCaprio and Tom Hanks, directed by Steven Spielberg.

Sully

On 15 January 2009, Captain Chesley "Sully" Sullenberger miraculously landed a passenger plane on the Hudson River over New York after a bird collision, saving the lives of 155 people. The incident became a media sensation, but an aviation investigation revealed a series of professional errors that eventually led to Captain Sully's retirement.

The circle

Mae Holland is a recent graduate starting her career at the world's most powerful internet company. The Circle allows you to send personal emails, social media presences, addresses, bank details and all your important data from anywhere in the world. Mae is fascinated by this modern world and all that the internet can offer people. But as she gets deeper and deeper into the details, she discovers what the giant corporation is really doing.
